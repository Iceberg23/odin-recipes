Odin's Lasagna

A delectable italian meat and cheese pie!

Ingredients

  • Pasta Sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Mozarella
  • Ricotta

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 450 degrees
  2. While oven is preheating, brown the ground beef
  3. Coat large dish with cooking spray
  4. Assemble lasagna
  5. Bake for 15 minutes
  6. Enjoy!
